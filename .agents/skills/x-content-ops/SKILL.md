# X Content Operations Skill

## Workflow

1. Identify the core thesis and intended audience.
2. Choose format: single post, thread, teardown, story, tutorial, contrarian take, product update, or reply.
3. Preserve facts and claims; do not invent experience.
4. Rewrite with platform-native language.
5. Remove obvious AI smell.
6. Produce variants when useful.
7. Provide brief notes on what changed and why.

## Anti-AI smell pass

Check for:

- Generic openings.
- Over-polished transitions.
- Empty grand claims.
- Unnatural symmetry.
- Corporate filler.
- Too many bullet points.
- No concrete examples.
- Fake certainty.

## Output

- Final post/thread.
- Optional hook variants.
- Optional short/medium/long versions.
- Style notes.
- Risk notes if claims need evidence.
