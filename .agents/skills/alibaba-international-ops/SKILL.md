# Alibaba International Station Operations Skill

## Required context

Before conclusions, inspect or request available data:

- Product list and product detail pages.
- Keyword data.
- Visitor data.
- Inquiry data.
- Category data.
- Promotion/ad data.
- Target countries and buyer personas.

If data is missing, produce a best-effort framework but label all data-dependent conclusions as unverified.

## Workflow

1. Define the business objective: exposure, clicks, visitors, inquiries, qualified inquiries, conversion, new product testing, category expansion, or sales follow-up.
2. Segment by product line, category, country, keyword cluster, source, and buyer intent.
3. Identify bottleneck in the funnel: exposure, click-through, visitor quality, inquiry conversion, inquiry quality, or sales response.
4. Review product title, attributes, images, detail content, category fit, MOQ, price band, certificates, and trust proof.
5. Convert findings into actions with expected metric impact.
6. Build a validation plan: before/after date range, target metric, control group if possible.
7. Produce a visual report if the task includes trends or portfolio decisions.

## Output format

For each recommendation:

- Evidence.
- Buyer intent.
- Bottleneck.
- Action.
- Expected impact.
- Validation metric.
- Risk.

## Red flags

- Do not invent Alibaba platform data.
- Do not recommend keyword stuffing.
- Do not optimize only titles when the bottleneck is detail-page trust, price, MOQ, or wrong category.
- Do not treat every visitor as equally valuable.
