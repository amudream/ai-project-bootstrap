# Deep Research Skill

## Procedure

1. Define the research question and decision it supports.
2. Prefer primary sources: official docs, papers, standards, source repositories, product docs, legal/regulatory sources where relevant.
3. Use current sources for anything that may have changed.
4. Separate facts, interpretations, and recommendations.
5. Cite sources for load-bearing claims.
6. Identify uncertainty, disagreement, and missing evidence.
7. End with practical implications for this project.

## Output

```markdown
## Research answer

## What is known

## What is uncertain

## Implications for this project

## Recommended decisions

## Sources
```
