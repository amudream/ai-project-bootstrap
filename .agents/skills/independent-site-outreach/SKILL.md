# Independent Site Outreach Skill

## Required context

- Supplier product catalog.
- Target industry and buyer persona.
- Prospect URL or company list.
- Country/region.
- MOQ, price band, certificates, customization, lead time.
- Outreach rules and brand voice.

## Workflow

1. Identify prospect type and product categories.
2. Extract evidence from the prospect’s public site or provided materials.
3. Match existing products to plausible needs.
4. Classify prospect fit and reason.
5. Draft personalized outreach with one clear CTA.
6. Prepare CRM fields and follow-up plan.
7. Flag compliance, deliverability, or evidence gaps.

## Output

- Prospect summary.
- Evidence from site/material.
- Product fit.
- Likely need or pain point.
- Outreach angle.
- First email.
- Follow-up sequence if requested.
- CRM fields.
- Risks or assumptions.

## Red flags

- Do not fake familiarity or personal experience.
- Do not invent prospect data.
- Do not produce spammy mass outreach.
- Do not bypass legal or platform restrictions.
