# Project Profile: Customer Support and CRM

## Objective

Build an AI-assisted workflow for customer demand extraction, support replies, objection handling, inquiry quality scoring, CRM notes, and customer feedback loops.

## Primary docs and skills

- `docs/domain-packs/customer-support-crm.md`
- `.agents/skills/customer-support-crm/SKILL.md`

## Required datasets

- Customer messages.
- Product/service catalog.
- Price/MOQ/lead time policy where applicable.
- Support policies.
- Existing CRM fields.

## Key metrics

- Response time.
- Resolution rate.
- Inquiry quality.
- Conversion stage movement.
- Repeated objections.
- Product request frequency.
- Escalation rate.

## Report visuals

- Demand cluster map.
- Objection heatmap.
- Response-time trend.
- Inquiry funnel.

## Forbidden behavior

- Do not promise unverified commercial terms.
- Do not expose internal notes to customers.
- Do not ignore escalation, complaint, safety, legal, refund, or warranty risk.
