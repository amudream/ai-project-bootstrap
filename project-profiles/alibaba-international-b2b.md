# Project Profile: Alibaba International Station B2B Operations

## Objective

Build an AI-assisted B2B export operations system for product posting, product optimization, keyword/category/visitor/inquiry analysis, promotion review, customer demand summary, and operation reporting.

## Primary docs and skills

- `docs/domain-packs/alibaba-international-b2b.md`
- `.agents/skills/alibaba-international-ops/SKILL.md`
- `docs/domain-packs/customer-support-crm.md` when inquiries or replies are involved
- `.agents/skills/customer-support-crm/SKILL.md`

## Required datasets

- Product export.
- Keyword performance export.
- Visitor export.
- Inquiry export.
- Category trend export if available.
- Promotion/ad export if used.

## Key metrics

- Exposure.
- Clicks.
- CTR.
- Visitors.
- Inquiry count.
- Qualified inquiry rate.
- Inquiry conversion rate.
- Country/category/product-line contribution.
- Cost per inquiry where ads exist.

## Report visuals

- Product funnel.
- Keyword opportunity matrix.
- Country heatmap.
- Category trend chart.
- Product portfolio matrix.
- Inquiry quality funnel.

## Forbidden behavior

- Do not invent platform metrics.
- Do not recommend keyword stuffing.
- Do not ignore buyer quality.
- Do not expose customer data in public reports.
