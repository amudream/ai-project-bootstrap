# Project Profile: Independent Site Prospecting and Outreach

## Objective

Build an AI-assisted workflow for prospect discovery, independent site analysis, product fit scoring, email outreach, follow-up sequencing, and CRM-ready export.

## Primary docs and skills

- `docs/domain-packs/independent-site-outreach.md`
- `.agents/skills/independent-site-outreach/SKILL.md`
- `docs/domain-packs/customer-support-crm.md` when replies start arriving

## Required datasets

- Product catalog.
- Prospect list or target market.
- Prospect website content.
- Buyer persona.
- Country/region constraints.
- Certifications, MOQ, price band, lead time, customization.

## Key metrics

- Prospect fit score.
- Contacted count.
- Open/reply rate if available.
- Qualified replies.
- Sample/quote requests.
- Objections.
- Conversion stage movement.

## Report visuals

- Prospect fit matrix.
- Market map.
- Outreach funnel.
- Objection heatmap.
- Email sequence performance trend.

## Forbidden behavior

- Do not invent prospect details.
- Do not create spam-like outreach.
- Do not fake relationship, purchase intent, or personal experience.
- Do not violate site or email compliance constraints.
