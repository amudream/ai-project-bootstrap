# Domain Pack: Customer Support and CRM

Use this pack for summarizing customer needs, replying to customers, extracting objections, building CRM notes, and converting support data into product or sales insights.

## Primary objectives

- Understand what the customer actually wants, not only what they typed.
- Produce clear, accurate, polite, and commercially useful replies.
- Capture structured CRM notes for future follow-up.
- Convert repeated questions and objections into product, documentation, and sales actions.

## Typical tasks

- Summarize customer demand.
- Draft customer replies.
- Extract quantity, target price, deadline, country, use case, product requirements, and objections.
- Classify inquiry quality.
- Prepare quotation information.
- Detect escalation, refund, complaint, warranty, or compliance issues.
- Build FAQ and response templates.

## Required inputs

- Original customer message.
- Product information.
- Price/MOQ/lead time rules.
- Company policy.
- Language and tone constraints.
- Known customer history if available.

## Response protocol

1. Identify the customer’s explicit request.
2. Infer possible hidden needs, but label them as inference.
3. Check whether answer requires policy, pricing, technical, legal, or human approval.
4. Draft a reply that answers directly before selling.
5. Ask only necessary follow-up questions.
6. Record CRM fields.
7. Suggest internal next action.

## CRM fields

- Customer name/company.
- Country/region.
- Product interest.
- Quantity / MOQ.
- Target price or budget.
- Use case.
- Deadline.
- Certification or compliance requirements.
- Objections.
- Urgency.
- Inquiry quality.
- Next action.
- Owner.
